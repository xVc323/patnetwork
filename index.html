03 / A SHORT NOTE

        
I like understanding things, then making them better.
I’m Pat. I move between engineering, AI, product questions, and business thinking. For me, they are different ways of getting to the same place: something practical that genuinely helps.
I care about why something should exist, who it helps, and how it fits into a real workflow. I like turning technical possibilities into practical tools and making complicated things easier to understand.
Some projects start with a problem I have. Others start with a technology I want to understand. I would rather finish a few things carefully than make a long list of promises. The ones that turn out well enough get shared openly.
